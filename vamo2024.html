VAMO 2024


VAMO 2024 will be held on April 8, 2024 from 3:15-6:45pm at Van Antwerp Middle School in Niskayuna, NY.

Competition Schedule and Format

Schedule:

3:15-4:00pm: Introduction and Instructions
4:00-4:30pm: Individual Round
4:45-5:35pm: Team Round
5:45-6:15pm: Pizza
6:15-6:45pm: Closing Ceremony

Individual Round:

  1. The Individual Round features 3 divisions. 8th Grade Division: Problems geared to 8th graders, but 7th graders and younger can also participate! 7th Grade Division: Problems geared to 7th graders, but 6th graders and younger can also participate! 6th Grade Division: Problems geared to 6th graders and younger!

  2. Participants have 30 minutes to complete 15 problems.


  3. The answer to each problem is an integer.

  4. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 15 points.

Team Round:

  1. Each team can consist of 2-4 people from any school and any divisions of the Individual Round.

  2. Teams have 50 minutes to complete 25 problems. The Team Round does not feature divisons.

  3. The last 5 minutes of the round, teams recieve 3 estimation problems worth 1 point each. The top 3 teams that are closest to the answer recieves the point.

  4. The answer to each problem is an integer.

  5. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 28 points.

Scoring:

      1. Team scores are calculated by summing the average score of the team members' Individual Round with the team's Team Round score.
