Q: Who can I contact with further questions about VAMO?

Q: Is registering in advance necessassary to participate in VAMO?
A: Yes, we are requesting that all participants register in order to participate in VAMO. This is mainly so that everyone can have a team with others that they know and so that we know how many competitors will be taking each division of the Individual Round

Q: This is my first time doing any math competition. Which Individual Round should I take?
A: If this is your first time, welcome! In this case, we would recommend that you take the division that corresponds to your grade level in school. We try to make the individual rounds so that competitors in that grade level should be able to have the tools to solve most problems even if you have never previously participated in a math competition!

Q: Will food be provided?
A: Yes! Pizza and other snacks will be provided between the Team Round and Awards Ceremony.
