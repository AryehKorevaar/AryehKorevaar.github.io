Competition Schedule and Format

Individual Round:

  1. The Individual Round features two divisions: Division 1 and Division 2. Division 2 is guided towards elementary schoolers and those with minimal experience in competition math.

  2. Participants have 30 minutes to complete 20 problems.

  3. Each answer is an integer.

  4. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 20 points

Team Round:

  1. Each team can hold up to 4 people from both divisions of the Individual Round.

  2. Teams have 45 minutes to complete 30 problems. The Team Round does not feature divisons.

  3. Each answer is a positive integer.

  4. The last 5 minutes of the round, teams recieve 3 estimation problems worth 1 each. The team that is closest to the answer recieves the point.

  5. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 30 points.

Scoring:

      1. Team scores are calculated by summing the average score of the team members' Individual Round with the team's Team Round score.
      2. VAMO 2022 results and prize information will be released by June 9th, 2022 (2 weeks after the competition).
