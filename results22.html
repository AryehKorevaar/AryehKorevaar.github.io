VAMO 2022 Results

VAMO 2022 was held May 26th, 2022 from 3:15pm to 5:15pm in-person at Van Antwerp Middle School. It consisted of one 30 minute Individual Round with 2 divisions and 20 short answer questions worth 1 point each and one 45 minute Team Round with 27 regular short answer problems worth 1 point each and 3 estimation problems worth 1 point each.
