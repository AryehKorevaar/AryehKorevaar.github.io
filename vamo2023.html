VAMO 2023


VAMO 2023 was held on Monday, March 27, 2023 in-person at Van Antwerp Middle School from 3:30 to 6:30pm and consisted of a 30 minute Individual Round followed by a 50 minute Team Round. A total of 58 students from Van Antwerp Middle School, Iroquois Middle School, and from various Elementary Schools participated at VAMO 2023. Thank you to everyone who came and made VAMO 2023 a great success!

Competition Schedule and Format

Schedule:

3:30-4:00pm: Go over competition format and any questions
4:00-4:30pm: Individual Round
4:45-5:35pm: Team Round
5:40-6:10pm: Pizza
6:10-6:30pm: Results and Prizes

Individual Round:

  1. The Individual Round features 3 divisions. Division 1: Problems geared to 8th graders, but 7th graders and younger can also participate! Division 2: Problems geared to 7th graders, but 6th graders and younger can also participate! Division 3: Problems geared to 6th graders and younger!

  2. Participants have 30 minutes to complete 15 problems.


  3. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 15 points.

Team Round:

  1. Each team can consist of 2-4 people from any school and any divisions of the Individual Round.

  2. Teams have 50 minutes to complete 25 problems. The Team Round does not feature divisons.

  3. The last 5 minutes of the round, teams recieve 3 estimation problems worth 1 point each. The team that is closest to the answer recieves the point.

  4. Each problem is worth 1 point, for a maximum of 28 points.

Scoring:

      1. Team scores are calculated by summing the average score of the team members' Individual Round with the team's Team Round score.
